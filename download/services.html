What is Clay Bar Decontamination?

Clay bar decontamination is a process used to remove contaminants from your car's paint that regular washing can't eliminate. Over time, your vehicle's surface can accumulate things like brake dust, tree sap, industrial fallout, and road tar. These contaminants can make your paint feel rough and can cause long-term damage if left untreated. Clay barring is an essential part of maintaining your cars look, value and longevity. By removing the embedded contaminants from your paint, it will leave it smoother, slicker and enhances its shine. This prepares your vehicle perfectly for a ceramic coating.
