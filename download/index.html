Yes, at Clarkes Auto Detailing, we strive for ultimate customer comfort, and satisfaction. Detailing is a luxury service, and part of the experience is the fact that it happens in the comfort of your own home. However, I also offer a service in which you can bring your vehicle to me.

Timings vary with a detail and are based on many factors, such as the condition of the vehicle and the package selected. At Clarkes Auto Detailing, we aim for quality over quantity to ensure your vehicle gets the care it deserves.

Car detailing is far superior to your local car wash in many ways. Firstly, car washes are large-scale operations, who aim to serve as many customers, and paying for this in the quality of their work meaning scratches and swirls are very common. On the other hand, detailing is the act of a professional with top of the line chemicals and techniques honed over many years of practice and work carefully reviving your car back to a showroom condition.
