About Us:

At Clarkes Auto Detailing, we pride ourselves on offering premium car detailing services tailored to your vehicle's unique needs. Located in the heart of Fleet, Hampshire, we specialise in exterior and interior interior detailing, ensuring your car looks its absolute best, down to the final detail.

I created Clarkes Auto Detailing out of a true passion for cars and a commitment to excellence. My journey began as a kid who loved to clean his bike at every opportunity. Combining this with my love for cars and motor vehicles, I began detailing friends, and families cars and building my skillset as a car detailer. As a lifelong car enthusiast, I, the owner, always dreamt of creating a business that combines my love for automobiles with my keen eye for detail. This passion led me to establish Clarkes Auto Detailing in Fleet, where we strive to deliver outstanding results with every service we provide. Our team is dedicated to reviving your vehicle to a showroom finish, using high-quality products and meticulous techniques honed over years of experience. We believe that a well-detailed car not only looks incredible but also enhances your driving experience. Our clients trust us for our expertise, our commitment to exceptional customer care, and our unwavering attention to the smallest details. We look forward to making your car shine like never before.
